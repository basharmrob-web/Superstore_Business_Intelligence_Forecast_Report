# Sales Trend and Forecasting Analysis

## Overview
This section outlines the predictive forecasting and time-series trends generated for the Global Superstore dataset to project future business performance and guide strategic planning.

---

## Forecasting Methodology & Approach
* **Time-Series Decomposition:** Analyzing historical monthly and quarterly sales to detect underlying seasonal patterns and cyclical components.
* **Predictive Modeling:** Applying forecasting models within Power BI to project future revenue and sales volume trends.
* **Horizon:** Short-to-medium-term projections covering future quarters to anticipate demand fluctuations.

---

## Key Forecasting Insights & Projections
1. **Growth Trajectory:** Overall enterprise sales demonstrate a consistent upward trend across consecutive operating years.
2. **Seasonality Impact:** Noticeable sales spikes observed during specific quarters (typically overlapping with holiday seasons and year-end retail peaks).
3. **Category Projections:** 
   * **Technology:** Projected to maintain leadership in high-value sales growth.
   * **Furniture & Office Supplies:** Expected to show steady, stable demand cycles with predictable recurring orders.
