# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Summary

## Overview
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was conducted to understand the underlying patterns, trends, and sales performance across products, regions, and customer segments in the Global Superstore dataset.

The objective of this phase was to explore historical sales data before diving into forecasting insights and advanced dashboard development.

---

## Dataset Overview
* **Total Transactions / Rows:** Analyzed across thousands of global records.
* **Key Dimensions:** Region, Category, Sub-Category, Segment, and Order Date.
* **Core Metrics:** Total Sales, Total Profit, Quantity, and Discount.

---

## Key Observations & Findings
1. **Sales Concentration:** Certain regions (such as the West and East) contribute significantly higher revenue compared to others.
2. **Product Categories:** **Technology** leads in terms of overall sales value and profitability, followed closely by Furniture and Office Supplies.
3. **Customer Segments:** The Consumer segment forms the largest share of the customer base and total order volume.
